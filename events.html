Wedding Coverage

Creative Candids offers full service wedding coverage.

Wedding coverage includes:
  • Engagement Portrait Session
  • Wallet prints of engagement portrait will be provided for all guests with information to access wedding photos on web.
  • UNLIMITED coverage on wedding day. I will arrive when you want me, and do not leave until the bride and groom leave.
  • Collage with best images posted within one week of the wedding.
  • Online wedding album will be posted within two weeks of wedding.
  • All proofs will be posted online within four weeks.
  • $1000 Album or Print Credit, contact me for specifics on prices.
  • Albums are high quality leather bound albums with full page images. There is no additional cost for black and white or colorized images.
Cost:
  • Wedding coverage costs $1500 to be paid in two installments.
  • The first installment of $500 is to be paid at the time of signing the contract, and is required to book the date.
  • The second installment of $1000 is due two weeks before the wedding.
